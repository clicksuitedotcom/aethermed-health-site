Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AetherMed collects, uses, discloses, stores, and protects personal information when you visit the website, submit the free assessment form, communicate with AetherMed, or use AetherMed coordination services. Medical information is sensitive and will be handled with care for medical review and coordination purposes.

1. Information We Collect

Contact and identity information, such as first name, last name, email, WhatsApp, country, nationality, passport or travel information where needed, and preferred language.

Medical and health information, such as diagnosis, condition summary, symptoms, medical history, medications, allergies, test results, imaging reports, insurance documents, treatment goals, and doctor or hospital records.

Service and travel information, such as specialty interest, hospital preferences, budget range, timeline, visa needs, accommodation needs, travel dates, local logistics, and follow-up needs.

Communications, such as messages, emails, forms, call notes, translations, summaries, feedback, and service requests.

Website and technical information, such as device/browser information, usage logs, analytics, and cookies if enabled on the website.

2. How We Use Information

Provide the free assessment and respond to inquiries.

Review, organize, translate, and summarize medical records for coordination purposes.

Match users with possible hospitals, departments, doctors, remote consultation options, and treatment pathways in China.

Coordinate appointments, hospital invitation letters where available, travel support, accommodation, local logistics, interpretation, payment support, document packages, and follow-up communication.

Maintain records, improve services, protect security, prevent misuse, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

3. Consent and Sensitive Information

By submitting information to AetherMed, you consent to AetherMed collecting, using, disclosing, transferring, and storing that information for assessment and coordination purposes described in this Policy.

Because medical and health information is sensitive, AetherMed will use it only for relevant medical review, coordination, document preparation, communication, and service administration unless otherwise authorized or required by law.

You may withdraw consent by contacting AetherMed. Withdrawal may limit or prevent AetherMed from providing assessment or coordination services.

4. Sharing Information

AetherMed may share information with third parties only as reasonably needed for assessment, coordination, service delivery, legal compliance, or user-authorized purposes.

Hospitals, doctors, clinics, laboratories, imaging centers, pharmacies, rehabilitation providers, and other healthcare providers.

Medical advisors, case managers, translators, interpreters, travel and accommodation providers, payment processors, insurers, visa-support contacts, technology vendors, and professional advisors.

Family members or representatives authorized by the user.

Public authorities, regulators, courts, or emergency responders where required or permitted by law, or where necessary to protect health, safety, rights, or property.

AetherMed does not sell personal medical information.

5. Cross-Border Transfer

AetherMed operates across international contexts, including Canada and China, and users may be located in other countries. Information may be transferred to, accessed from, or stored in countries other than the user's country of residence for medical review, coordination, communication, provider matching, document preparation, and service administration.

Where required, AetherMed will seek appropriate consent or use appropriate contractual, administrative, technical, or organizational measures for cross-border handling of personal information.

6. Retention, Safeguards, and User Rights

AetherMed keeps personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for assessment, service delivery, follow-up coordination, legal compliance, recordkeeping, dispute resolution, and legitimate business purposes.

AetherMed uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or loss. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Depending on applicable law, users may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, withdrawal of consent, restriction of use, explanation of processing, or information about disclosures. Requests may be sent to info@aethermed.health.

7. Children, Cookies, Updates, and Contact

If information relates to a minor or a person who cannot provide consent independently, the submitting person confirms that they are the parent, guardian, lawful representative, or otherwise authorized person.

The website may use basic cookies, analytics, or similar technologies to support functionality, understand usage, improve content, and protect security.

AetherMed may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on the website or otherwise made available.

Privacy questions, access requests, correction requests, deletion requests, or consent-withdrawal requests may be sent to: info@aethermed.health.

中文

隐私政策

本隐私政策说明 AetherMed 在您访问网站、提交免费评估表单、与 AetherMed 沟通或使用协调服务时，如何收集、使用、披露、存储和保护个人信息。医疗信息属于敏感信息，AetherMed 将谨慎处理，并仅用于医疗审核和协调目的。

1. 我们收集的信息

联系和身份信息，例如名、姓、邮箱、WhatsApp、所在国家、国籍、在需要时的护照或旅行信息以及语言偏好。

医疗和健康信息，例如诊断、情况简述、症状、病史、用药、过敏史、检查结果、影像报告、保险文件、治疗目标以及医生或医院记录。

服务和旅行信息，例如咨询专科方向、医院偏好、预算范围、时间安排、签证需求、住宿需求、旅行日期、当地后勤安排和随访需求。

沟通信息，例如消息、邮件、表单、通话记录、翻译、摘要、反馈和服务请求。

网站和技术信息，例如设备或浏览器信息、使用日志、分析数据以及网站启用时的 cookies。

2. 我们如何使用信息

提供免费评估，并回复咨询。

为协调目的审核、整理、翻译和总结病历资料。

根据用户情况匹配中国的医院、科室、医生、远程诊疗选项和治疗路径。

协调预约、医院邀请函、旅行支持、住宿、当地后勤、翻译、支付支持、文件包和后续沟通。

维护记录、改善服务、保护安全、防止滥用、履行法律义务和解决争议。

3. 同意与敏感信息

您向 AetherMed 提交信息，即表示您同意 AetherMed 为本政策所述评估和协调目的收集、使用、披露、转移和存储相关信息。

由于医疗和健康信息属于敏感信息，AetherMed 仅会将其用于相关医疗审核、协调、文件准备、沟通和服务管理，除非另获授权或法律另有要求。

您可联系 AetherMed 撤回同意。撤回同意可能限制或导致 AetherMed 无法继续提供评估或协调服务。

4. 信息共享

AetherMed 可在评估、协调、服务交付、法律合规或用户授权目的所合理需要的范围内，与第三方共享信息，包括：

医院、医生、诊所、实验室、影像中心、药房、康复机构及其他医疗服务提供方。

医学顾问、个案经理、翻译、口译、旅行和住宿服务商、支付处理方、保险机构、签证支持联系人、技术供应商和专业顾问。

经用户授权的家属或代表。

法律要求或允许时的公共机关、监管机构、法院或应急响应人员，或为保护健康、安全、权利或财产所必要的情形。

AetherMed 不出售个人医疗信息。

5. 跨境传输

AetherMed 在国际场景下运营，包括加拿大和中国，用户也可能位于其他国家。为医疗审核、协调、沟通、服务匹配、文件准备和服务管理目的，信息可能被传输至、访问自或存储于用户居住国以外的国家。不同国家的隐私法律可能不同。

在法律要求的情况下，AetherMed 将寻求适当同意，或采取适当的合同、管理、技术或组织措施处理跨境个人信息。

6. 信息保存、安全措施与用户权利

AetherMed 仅在免费评估、服务交付、后续协调、法律合规、记录保存、争议解决和合理商业目的所需期间保存个人信息。

AetherMed 采用合理的管理、技术和组织措施，旨在保护个人信息免遭未经授权的访问、使用、披露、变更或丢失。但任何电子传输或存储方式都无法保证绝对安全。

根据适用法律，用户可能有权请求访问个人信息、更正不准确信息、删除信息、撤回同意、限制使用、了解处理情况或查询披露情况。相关请求可发送至 info@aethermed.health。

7. 未成年人、Cookies、政策更新与联系

如信息涉及未成年人或无法独立同意的人，提交人确认其为父母、监护人、合法代表或其他有权代表该人的人员。

网站可能使用基础 cookies、分析或类似技术，用于支持网站功能、了解使用情况、改善内容和保护安全。

AetherMed 可不时更新本隐私政策。更新版本将在网站发布或以其他方式提供。

隐私问题、访问请求、更正请求、删除请求或撤回同意请求可发送至：info@aethermed.health。
